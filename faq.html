Your Questions,
Answered.

Clear answers to common questions about our web design and development process.

How much does a website cost?

Website pricing depends on your requirements like number of pages, design complexity, and features. Basic websites start at affordable rates, and exact pricing is shared after understanding your needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 5–10 working days. Larger or custom projects may take slightly longer depending on features and revisions.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Yes ✅ All websites are 100% mobile-responsive and work smoothly on mobiles, tablets, and desktops.

Do you provide websites for schools and coaching institutes?

Yes. We specialize in websites for schools, coaching institutes, and educational organizations, including admission enquiry forms and student-friendly layouts.

Will my website be SEO-friendly?

Yes. Every website is built with basic SEO structure, fast loading speed, mobile optimization, and clean code to help rank better on Google.

Do you provide domain & hosting?

We can guide you in purchasing domain and hosting or help manage them if required. You stay in full control.

Can I update my website later?

Yes. You can request updates anytime. We also provide maintenance and support services if you need regular updates.

Do you redesign old websites?

Yes. We redesign outdated websites into modern, fast, and professional designs without losing your existing content.

Is there any advance payment?

Yes. A small advance is required to start the project, and the remaining payment is taken after design approval or before final delivery.
